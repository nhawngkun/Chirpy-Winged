Movement

Joystick (Left): Use the left joystick to move your character around the circular arena
Drag your finger on the joystick to walk in any direction
Release to stop moving immediately
Your character will automatically rotate to face the direction you're moving

Throwing Cakes

Joystick (Right): Use the right joystick to aim and throw cakes
When you're carrying a cake, an aim arrow and line will appear
Drag the right joystick in the direction you want to throw
Release the joystick to launch the cake toward the target
You can throw cakes at cake boxes to score points or at chefs to destroy them!

Collecting Cakes

Cakes spawn randomly around the arena with a glowing, rotating animation
Walk close to a cake (within pickup radius) to automatically collect it
When you pick up a cake, a visual model appears on your character's head
You can only carry one cake at a time

Delivering Cakes

Look for the cake boxes positioned around the arena
Walk into a cake box while carrying a cake to deposit it and score points
Alternatively, you can throw the cake into the box from a distance
Each successful delivery increases your score!
